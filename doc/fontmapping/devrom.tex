\begin{document}
Two new commands \texttt{\textbackslash skt} and \texttt{\textbackslash iast}
enable writing IAST text in Devanāgarī, to have it rendered as IAST in the PDF.

\noindent\fbox{\parbox{\textwidth}{
\texttt{Example of Devanāgarī Text:\\
\textbackslash skt\{}\skt{रामो राजमणि सदा विजयते रामं रमेशं भजे।}\texttt{\}\\
Example of IAST Text written in Devanāgarī:\\
\textbackslash iast\{}\skt{रामो राजमणि सदा विजयते रामं रमेशं भजे।}\texttt{\}}
}}

$$\downarrow$$

\noindent\fbox{\parbox{\textwidth}{
Example of Devanāgarī Text:\\
\skt{रामो राजमणि सदा विजयते रामं रमेशं भजे।}\\
Example of IAST Text written in Devanāgarī:\\
\iast{रामो राजमणि सदा विजयते रामं रमेशं भजे।}
}}
